Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2025

1. Introduction

ScionTel ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information through our services and products.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number)
  • Payment and transaction information
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Provide and maintain our services
  2. Process transactions and payments
  3. Improve our products and services
  4. Communicate with users
  5. Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Payment processors (Paddle.com) - As our payment processor, Paddle acts as a data controller for transaction data and is subject to Paddle's Privacy Policy available at https://paddle.com/privacy
  • Service providers and business partners
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We have entered into a Data Sharing Addendum with Paddle which governs the processing of personal data. This addendum is available at https://paddle.com/legal/data-sharing-addendum

5. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls
  • PCI DSS compliance through our payment processor Paddle

Data is retained for the following periods:

  • Transaction data: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • User account data: Until account deletion request
  • Marketing data: 3 years from last interaction

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

For requests related to payment processing data, please contact Paddle directly at privacy@paddle.com
