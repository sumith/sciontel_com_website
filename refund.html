Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2025

1. General Refund Policy

ScionTel offers refunds in accordance with the following conditions:

  • Refunds are processed through Paddle.com, our payment processor
  • Refund requests must be made within 30 days of purchase
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method

2. Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  1. The product is defective or not as described
  2. You experience technical issues that prevent product usage
  3. You accidentally purchased the wrong product

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Products purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Products that have been substantially used
  • Products purchased during sales or promotions

4. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@sciontel.com
  2. Provide your order details and reason for refund
  3. Wait for our response (typically within 2 business days)

5. Chargebacks and Disputes

As Paddle is our Merchant of Record, all chargebacks are handled through their system:

  • Chargeback fee: £20, $20, or €20 depending on transaction currency
  • Disputes are handled through Paddle's resolution center
  • We may provide evidence to contest invalid chargebacks
  • Excessive chargebacks (>1% of transactions) may result in account suspension

Paddle's chargeback protection includes:

  1. Automated fraud detection
  2. Dispute resolution assistance
  3. Chargeback prevention tools

6. Refund Timeframes and Guarantees

Paddle guarantees refund processing within:

  • 5 business days for credit/debit card payments
  • 7 business days for PayPal payments
  • 10 business days for bank transfers

Refund status can be tracked through:

  1. Your Paddle account dashboard
  2. Email notifications from Paddle
  3. Our support team at support@sciontel.com
